We have provided interior design, radiation shielding and fitting-out works, design of electrical circuit, installation & repair of electrical installation for hospitals and medical centres in Hong Kong, Macau and Shenzhen since 1984.

We also have lead sheets, lead glasses, lead bricks, automatic door system, tailor made chiller system, routers, servers, optical fibres and CAT6 cables for sell.

Our customers include public hospitals, private hospitals, clinical, medical centres and medical machine suppliers. We have over thirty experienced technicians for design, installation, repair & maintenance services for Air-conditioning System, Chiller System, Heat Exchange System, Automatic Door System, CCTV System and Network Solutions, especially for installation of X-Ray, Digital X-Ray, CT, PET CT, MRI, Radiation Therapy machine, Linear Accelerator, etc. We also provide annual maintenance for CCTV System, Air-Conditioning System, Automatic Door System and Network Solutions.
